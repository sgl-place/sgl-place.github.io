DCR Games

These games require an old-ass version of Flash (Shockwave Player) that's no longer supported by Chrome or Firefox. Internet Explorer worryingly still includes and enables it by default, or you can download the plugin for use in other browsers like Chromium, Opera, or Pale Moon (my personal preference).
